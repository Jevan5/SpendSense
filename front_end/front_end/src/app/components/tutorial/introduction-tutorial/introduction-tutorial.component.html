Welcome to SpendSense! Thank you for joining the SpendSense community, where we can help you track your expenses to let you better manage your finances. Receipts Expense information typically comes in the form of receipts. Receipts contain items, which have a name and price. To track your expenses, these receipts can be recorded, and each item can be associated with categorical information to break down your expenses. Tags Knowing how much you spent over a period time is not enough to properly evaluate your spending. A more powerful way to track your spending is to categorize your spending, and observe your spending in each category. This can be accomplished by associating a "tag" with each of the items on your receipts.
